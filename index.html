butterfly recordings on Australian map

This visualization uses butterfly data from https://www.ala.org.au/ The butterfly species are Heteronympha merope and Pieris rapae. The definition of wing aspect ratio in this visualization is the (width of the bounding box )/(height of the bounding box) The size of circle indicates the butterfly wing aspect ratio
